ABOUT US

Who are we?

At Ibikunle CardioCare Alliance, we understand that every heartbeat matters. Founded by Mr. Olamide Ibikunle, with a passion for enhancing patient outcomes, our alliance brings together a team of dedicated healthcare professionals committed to providing comprehensive cardiovascular care of the highest standard.
With a focus on preventive care and advanced education, we strive to empower vulnerable adults aged 50 and above to lead heart-healthy lives and overcome cardiovascular challenges with confidence.
Join us in our mission to redefine cardiovascular healthcare and make a meaningful difference in the lives of vulnerable adults in Nigeria and Africa as a whole. Together, we can build a healthier, heart-strong future for generations to come.

Mission and Vision Statement

Our Mission
To empower adults aged 50 and above to embrace heart health through comprehensive education, and innovative research initiatives. We are committed to reducing the burden of cardiovascular diseases by fostering awareness, providing quality education, and advocating for policies that promote heart wellness for all.

Our Vision
Our vision is a world where every heartbeat thrives, where cardiovascular diseases are preventable, and where individuals lead vibrant, heart-healthy lives. We aspire to be a catalyst for change, driving innovation, collaboration, and equitable access to quality education, to create a future where hearts beat strong, resilient, and full of vitality.
